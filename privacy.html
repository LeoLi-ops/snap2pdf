Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SnapToPDF handles your information.

1. Information We Process

SnapToPDF processes document images, generated PDFs, and related metadata (for example document title and timestamps) to provide scan and export features.

2. On-Device Processing

Document capture, correction, enhancement, and PDF generation are performed on your device by default.

3. Permissions

  • Camera: Required for document scanning.
  • Photo Library (optional): Used when importing images.

You can manage permissions anytime in iOS Settings.

4. Data Storage

Your scans and PDFs are stored in your app container on your device unless you choose to export/share them.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only when you explicitly choose to export or share files using system sharing options.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete workspace records and history records directly inside the app. Uninstalling the app removes local app-container data managed by iOS.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: leoliops@outlook.com
