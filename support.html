FAQ

Why is my scan not detected correctly?

Try better lighting, keep the document edges fully visible, and hold the camera steady.

Where are my files stored?

Generated PDFs are saved in your app Documents directory and can be shared via the export options.

How can I restore purchases?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchases in the app.
